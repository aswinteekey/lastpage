About us

What is LastPage ?

LastPage Exchange is an innovative web application designed to facilitate the exchange of used books through a coin-based system. Users can effortlessly trade their pre-owned books by utilizing virtual coins provided within the platform. Additionally, LastPage Exchange offers a unique feature where users can post reviews about their books, enticing other members to engage in exchanges. The platform boasts a user-friendly interface, allowing individuals to browse, list, and search for books with ease. Leveraging advanced algorithms, LastPage Exchange ensures fair and efficient matches between users, enhancing the overall trading experience. By combining the convenience of online trading with the value of peer reviews, LastPage Exchange aims to create a vibrant community dedicated to sustainable book exchange and knowledge sharing.

How it works ?

After creating a user profile, users can upload a review of a book they own as in a Facebook post. If they like any other book review, they can ask to borrow it by trading coins. These coins can be bought from the platform or acquired through trading books. After accepting the borrowing request, they can hand over the book at a specific point provided by the platform. Borrower can upload current images of book and owner can accept images for security purpose Once this verification of the books is completed, the coins are transferred from the borrower to the owner's account to ensure the safety of the books. After completing the book reading period the book is returned to the owner and the coins to the other user
